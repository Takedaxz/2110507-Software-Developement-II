About Car Expo 2024

Car Expo 2024 is the largest car exhibition in the country, showcasing the latest models and innovations in the automotive industry.

Event Date

The Car Expo 2024 will take place from May 1st to May 31st, 2024.

About the Venue

The Car Expo 2024 will take place at IMPACT Arena, Exhibition and Convention Center, which is a state-of-the-art convention center equipped with modern facilities to accommodate exhibitors and visitors.

Highlights of the Event

At Car Expo 2024, visitors can expect exciting events such as car launches, test drives, and interactive workshops. Don't miss the opportunity to experience the future of the automotive industry!
